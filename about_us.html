ABOUT US

Stria is a team of 14 high school students, determined to tackle the challenges of blindness.

Stria began in the summer of 2017 when a group of 4 high school students became interested in blindness. Now, Stria is a team of 14 high schoolers, dedicated to tackling blindness in the Bay Area.
