ABOUT US

The Stria Team is a group of highly motivated, and specialized students, working almost exclusively on our own time to create change in our local community. Founded by four high-schoolers in 2017, our team has expanded to include 3 subteams and 11 members. Stria Labs is also a registered C-Corp.

Stria began in the summer of 2017 when a group of 4 high school students became interested in blindness. Now, Stria is a team of 14 high schoolers, dedicated to tackling blindness in the Bay Area.
