ABOUT US

Stria is an early stage start-up devoted to creating a technological ecosystem for blind individuals, harnessing both the power of design and engineering and the technology in our laptops and phones. Our current flagship product, the Stria band, is a low-profile smart-waistband designed to ensure that the visually impaired remain safe from collisions with high-speed traffic.

WHAT WE DO

Bleeding Edge

Stria Labs has developed new technologies to tackle the problems associated with blidness. From our patented band to our industry changing Airflow 8 technology or even our proprietary detection algorithm, we reorient expectations of what is possible.

User Focused

At Stria, the user is at the center of everything. We've individually spoken to 200+ visually impaired individuals, and collaborated with dozens of blind centers.

Utility

Stria Labs cares about creating world-changing technology — that’s why every product is rigorously tested and built with quality. We build strategic relationships with everyone on our supply chain to make sure our product deserves your trust.
