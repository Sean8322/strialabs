About Us

Stria is a team of 14 high school students, determined to tackle the challenges of blindness. Stria began in the summer of 2017 when a group of four high school students became interested in blindness. Now, Stria is a team of 14 high schoolers, dedicated to tackling blindness in the Bay Area.

Team Members

Maddie Park, Noah Tavares, Max Shen, Grace Langner, Nikhil Suresh, Nicolaas Lit, Isabel Alvarez, Pranav Ram, Amit Singh, Sean Cheong, Justin Zhang, Megan Fong, Wesley Shapiro. Website by Alice Ge.
